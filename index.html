Quick Troubleshooting

  • Messages not sending: Check internet connection, restart app, or re-login.
  • Contacts missing: Ensure Contacts permission is allowed in device settings and refresh the app.
  • Unable to log in / OTP issues: Verify phone number and network; try OTP resend.
  • Media won’t upload: Confirm Photos permission and sufficient network/data.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Why does Dodm ask for Contacts permission?

To help you find which of your contacts already use Dodm and to make connecting easier. We only use contact data for matching; we do not sell or share your contacts.

Does Dodm store my photos?

Photos and media you share in chats are uploaded to our servers to deliver them to recipients. We do not access your photo gallery without permission.

How do I delete my account?

Open Profile → Settings → Delete Account inside the app. Deleting your account removes your personal data from our system.

How can I change my profile information?

Go to Profile → Edit Profile to update your display name and photo.
